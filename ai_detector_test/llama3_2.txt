Although much of Maui's food scene is quick service and takeout, I discovered a local Hawaiian gem called Birdhouse, offering an entirely vegan menu. For $40 per person, they presented a variety of dishes, each with detailed ingredient lists. For fellow vegans impressed by this level of vegan options in Hawaii, we'll share a tip - look up Vegan Gangsters Hawaii.
